PARTNER WITH MINISHOT

Invest in the next generation of Nigerian creators
Every breakthrough begins with a small investment in time, resources, or belief. At Minishot, we're building a community that invests in young Nigerian creators before they're "proven" by traditional metrics.

We support students and recent graduates working on creative, tech, research, and venture projects through micro-grants and structured support. Beyond funding, our fellows need mentorship, connections, accountability, and someone who believes in their potential.

As a partner, you're not just funding projects. You're backing the kind of ambitious, curious people who will shape Nigeria's creative and innovation landscape. You're giving them permission to focus, to experiment, to fail and try again.

What Makes Minishot Different

  • Low-overhead, high-trust: We keep admin lean so more resources go directly to creators
  • Flexible support: Not every project needs the same thing. We adapt to individual needs
  • Progress over perfection: We value learning and iteration, not just finished products
  • Community-first: Fellows support each other, creating a network that lasts beyond the program

What You Get as a Partner

  • Direct connection to talented, driven young Nigerians
  • Regular updates on fellow progress and achievements
  • Early access to emerging talent for hiring or collaboration
  • Recognition in our community and communications
  • Opportunity to shape the program's direction and growth

  • Creative Projects: Film, music, writing, design, digital art
  • Tech & Engineering: Apps, hardware, open-source tools
  • Research: Independent research, data projects, academic exploration
  • Social Impact: Community initiatives, education tools, advocacy
  • Entrepreneurship: Early-stage ventures, product prototypes

The Cost of Inaction

Right now, talented young Nigerians are choosing between their creative ambitions and paying rent. They're abandoning promising projects because they can't afford to continue. They're delaying their potential because no one's willing to bet on them early.

A small investment now can unlock years of impact.
